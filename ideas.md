# Design Philosophy: Sovereign Authority

## Chosen Approach: Brutalist Authority & Abstract Geometry
We are moving away from the "retail trading" aesthetic (blue gradients, charts, men in suits) towards a "Private Order" and "Strategic Doctrine" aesthetic. This design is intended to repel impulsive retail traders and attract disciplined, serious operators.

## Core Principles
1.  **Authority, Not Appeal:** The design does not try to "sell" or "convince." It states a doctrine. It is confident, imposing, and indifferent to approval.
2.  **Psychological Containment:** The interface feels like a contained environment—a sanctuary for deep work. Dark, immersive, and focused.
3.  **Structural, Not Superficial:** Visuals represent the *structure* of the market and the mind, not surface-level price action.
4.  **Ritualistic Interaction:** User interactions (hover states, transitions) should feel deliberate and weighted, not bouncy or gamified.

## Color Philosophy
*   **Deep Obsidian Black (#0B0D10):** The void, the unknown, the subconscious. The canvas upon which sovereignty is built.
*   **Burnished Bronze (#8B6B3E):** Alchemy, value, ancient wisdom. Used for borders, accents, and key typography. It implies enduring worth, not flashy wealth.
*   **Graphite (#161A20):** Structure, solidity, the "concrete" of the mental fortress. Used for cards and sections.
*   **Controlled Ivory (#EAE7DF):** Clarity, illumination, the conscious mind. Used for text, but slightly muted to reduce eye strain in the dark environment.
*   **Deep Oxblood Red (#5A1F1F):** Risk, danger, the "Rubedo" stage of alchemy. Used very sparingly for alerts or critical emphasis.

## Layout Paradigm
*   **Architectural & Brutalist:** Hard edges, distinct borders, visible grids. No rounded corners (radius: 0px).
*   **Asymmetric Balance:** Avoids perfect centering to create tension and dynamic energy.
*   **Macro Visuals:** Large, abstract geometric forms that dominate sections, representing psychological concepts rather than literal trading objects.

## Signature Elements
1.  **The Crown & Geometry:** Abstract representations of sovereignty (crowns) and market structure (lines, grids) merged into singular icons.
2.  **Bronze Borders:** 1px borders that define space and separate content, glowing faintly.
3.  **Serif Typography:** Use of *Cormorant Garamond* for headings to evoke history, wisdom, and "doctrine."

## Interaction Philosophy
*   **Weighty Transitions:** Hover effects are slow and smooth (duration-500), implying mass and significance.
*   **No Bounce:** Animations are linear or ease-out, never elastic. Authority does not bounce.
*   **Focus States:** Inputs and interactive elements glow with a bronze aura when active, symbolizing attention and focus.

## Typography System
*   **Headings:** *Cormorant Garamond* (Serif). Elegant, authoritative, timeless. Used for all major titles and "doctrine" statements.
*   **Body:** *Inter* (Sans-serif). Clean, legible, neutral. Represents the objective reality of the market.
*   **Accents:** Uppercase, wide tracking (letter-spacing) for labels and navigation to create a "military" or "classified" feel.
